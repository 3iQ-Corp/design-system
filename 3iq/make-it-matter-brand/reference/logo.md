# Logo

The #MakeItMatter campaign uses two logos:

1. **#MakeItMatter campaign logo** — the stylised `#MakeItMatter` text with a hand-drawn underline beneath "Matter"
2. **3iQ corporate wordmark** — used alongside the campaign logo to maintain corporate identity

## #MakeItMatter logo

### Files

| Variant | File | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Navy (for light backgrounds) | `assets/logos/3iQ-MakeItMatter-Logo-Navy.svg` | SVG |
| Navy (raster) | `assets/logos/3iQ-MakeItMatter-Logo-Navy.png` | PNG |
| White (for dark backgrounds) | `assets/logos/3iQ-MakeItMatter-Logo-White.svg` | SVG |
| White (raster) | `assets/logos/3iQ-MakeItMatter-Logo-White.png` | PNG |

Additional formats (`.ai`, `.eps`) are available in the toolkit download for print use.

### Color

- **On dark surfaces:** use the White version
- **On light surfaces:** use the Navy version
- **One colour only.** Never apply the accent colors (yellow, green, orange) to the logo.

### Clearspace

- **Height of the hashtag (#)** on all four sides. Nothing crosses into that space.
- Clearspace scales with the logo.

### Minimum size

- **Minimum width:** `120px` on screen.
- Always ensure the hand-drawn underline remains legible.

### Never

- Never **remove the underline** beneath "Matter" — it is integral to the logo.
- Never **remove the hashtag (#)** — it is integral to the mark.
- Never **add word spaces** — it is `#MakeItMatter`, not `#Make It Matter`.
- Never **use a different highlight style** — the underline is specific to the logo.
- Never **apply accent colors** to the logo — Navy or White only.
- Never **distort, rotate, stretch, or mirror** the logo.
- Never **recreate** the logo by typing "#MakeItMatter" in a font.

## 3iQ corporate wordmark

The corporate 3iQ wordmark is used alongside the campaign logo to maintain brand hierarchy. Full rules are in the [corporate design system](../README.md).

- **File:** `assets/logos/3iq_logo.svg`
- **viewBox:** `0 0 96 30`
- **Fill:** `currentColor` — set CSS `color: #FDFEFA` on dark, `color: #080E23` on light.
- **Minimum width:** `80px`.

### Placement

- The 3iQ corporate logo appears in the top-left of pages.
- The #MakeItMatter logo typically appears as a campaign badge — often in the footer, as a section closer, or as a social media tag.
- Both logos can coexist on the same surface but should maintain their respective clearspace.
